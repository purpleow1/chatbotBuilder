# AskDoc Customer FAQ

## What is AskDoc?
AskDoc is an embeddable chatbot builder for small SaaS teams. Teams upload support documents, train a bot, test it inside the dashboard, and embed it on their website.

## Setup time
Most teams can create a basic bot in under 10 minutes after their documents are ready.

## Supported document types
AskDoc supports TXT, Markdown, PDF, DOCX, and CSV files.

## Widget installation
The widget is installed by copying a script snippet from the bot settings page and pasting it before the closing body tag of a website.

## Brand customization
Free workspaces use AskDoc branding. Pro workspaces can customize the widget color and remove AskDoc branding.

## Support handoff
If the bot cannot answer confidently from uploaded knowledge, it should show the fallback message and suggest contacting the support team at support@askdoc.example.
