AskDoc Refund Policy

Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. When a customer cancels, access remains active until the end of the current billing period.

Refunds are available within 14 days of the first paid subscription charge if the workspace has used fewer than 200 assistant messages.

Refunds are not available for renewal charges after the first billing period.

Business plan customers may request invoice adjustments by contacting billing@askdoc.example.

The standard support response time is 2 business days for Free users, 1 business day for Pro users, and 4 business hours for Business users.
