# AskDoc Use Cases

Positioning direction: AskDoc turns company knowledge into AI assistants for customers, teams, and workflows.

## Primary use cases

1. **Customer support assistant**  
   Answers FAQs, refund policies, troubleshooting steps, account questions, shipping info, onboarding docs, and product guides.

2. **Sales enablement chatbot**  
   Helps prospects understand pricing, compare plans, answer objections, explain integrations, and route high-intent leads.

3. **Internal company knowledge base**  
   Lets employees ask questions about policies, benefits, tools, processes, org structure, and internal docs instead of searching Notion, Drive, or Slack.

4. **Employee onboarding assistant**  
   Gives new hires fast answers about equipment requests, brand guides, team rituals, sales process, security rules, and where key resources live.

5. **Product documentation assistant**  
   Turns technical docs, API references, changelogs, and help articles into a conversational product expert.

6. **Education or course assistant**  
   Helps students, customers, or trainees ask questions based on course materials, handbooks, PDFs, lesson notes, and training content.

7. **HR policy assistant**  
   Answers questions about PTO, parental leave, expenses, remote work rules, performance reviews, benefits, and compliance policies.

8. **Legal and compliance knowledge assistant**  
   Helps teams search approved policies, regulatory guidance, contracts, templates, and internal procedures conversationally.

9. **Agency client portal assistant**  
   Lets agencies upload project docs, strategy decks, timelines, deliverables, and FAQs so clients can self-serve answers.

10. **E-commerce shopping assistant**  
    Embedded on a storefront to answer sizing, materials, shipping, product comparisons, care instructions, and returns.

11. **SaaS onboarding assistant**  
    Embedded inside an app to help users set up features, understand workflows, and find the right documentation.

12. **Real estate or property assistant**  
    Answers questions about listings, amenities, lease terms, neighborhood info, building rules, and application steps.

13. **Healthcare admin assistant**  
    Handles clinic FAQs such as appointment prep, insurance basics, office policies, forms, locations, and post-visit instructions.

14. **Event or conference assistant**  
    Helps attendees ask about schedules, speakers, venue maps, sponsor info, travel, sessions, and ticket policies.

15. **Franchise or multi-location knowledge assistant**  
    Gives stores access to operating manuals, brand guidelines, recipes, training docs, and location-level policies.

16. **Partner or reseller enablement bot**  
    Gives partners fast answers from sales decks, positioning docs, pricing rules, certification guides, and co-marketing materials.

17. **Developer support assistant**  
    Answers API questions, SDK setup, authentication, webhook behavior, code examples, and integration troubleshooting.

18. **Community or membership assistant**  
    Helps members understand rules, benefits, events, resources, renewal policies, and how to access gated materials.

19. **Recruiting assistant**  
    Answers candidate FAQs about roles, interview process, benefits, company culture, location, and application guidance.

20. **Operations playbook assistant**  
    Lets internal teams query SOPs, incident response steps, escalation paths, vendor docs, and recurring process instructions.

## Landing page framing

Lead with: **Build AI assistants from your company knowledge.**

Suggested first-page paths:

- **Customer support** - answer repetitive customer questions from approved docs.
- **Internal knowledge** - give employees one place to ask policy, process, and tool questions.
- **Sales and product enablement** - help prospects, partners, and users understand your product faster.
