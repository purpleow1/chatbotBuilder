# Landing Demo Conversation Variants

Generated variants for the landing-page demo chat. Each variant is a four-message conversation and is written for a positive customer-support or internal-knowledge resolution.

## Customer Support

### Setup troubleshooting

Sources: `setup-guide.md`, `status-lights.pdf`, `troubleshooting-faq.md`

**User:** The status light is amber after setup. Is that bad?

**Assistant:** Amber means an update is running. Leave it powered for three minutes, then reconnect if the light stays amber.

**User:** Will reconnecting erase my settings?

**Assistant:** No. A short reconnect keeps the saved profile. Only the 15-second factory reset clears the workspace.

### Delivery update

Sources: `shipping-policy.md`, `order-changes-faq.md`, `delivery-playbook.pdf`

**User:** Can I update the delivery address before my order ships?

**Assistant:** Yes. Use the change link in your order email before the shipping label is created.

**User:** How do I know it saved?

**Assistant:** The order page will show the new city and delivery window. You will also get an updated shipping email.

### Team starter kit

Sources: `team-onboarding-guide.pdf`, `starter-kit-guide.md`, `access-policy.md`

**User:** I bought the starter kit for my team. Where should they begin?

**Assistant:** Start with the quick-start checklist, then invite each teammate from Team access so progress is tracked.

**User:** Can they use it before our onboarding call?

**Assistant:** Yes. Core workspace access is active right away; the onboarding call is for advanced setup and best practices.

## Internal Knowledge

### New-hire equipment

Sources: `onboarding-checklist.md`, `equipment-policy.pdf`, `people-ops-sop.md`

**User:** How do I request a monitor for a new hire?

**Assistant:** Submit an equipment request at least five business days before the start date and choose Standard desk kit.

**User:** Who approves the request?

**Assistant:** The hiring manager approves it first. People Ops then handles shipping and updates the onboarding tracker.

### Help article workflow

Sources: `content-workflow.md`, `support-review-checklist.pdf`, `knowledge-style-guide.md`

**User:** What is the process for publishing a new help article?

**Assistant:** Draft it in the knowledge workspace, tag Support Review, then get product signoff before publishing.

**User:** What if it is an urgent correction?

**Assistant:** The support lead can approve same-day fixes after the product owner confirms the updated answer is accurate.

### Incident escalation

Sources: `incident-response-sop.md`, `severity-matrix.pdf`, `customer-comms-template.md`

**User:** Where do I report a customer-impacting incident?

**Assistant:** Open the incident form, set the severity, and add the affected customer segment before paging the owner.

**User:** Who needs to be looped in for Sev2?

**Assistant:** Notify the support lead, engineering owner, and comms owner within 15 minutes using the Sev2 template.
